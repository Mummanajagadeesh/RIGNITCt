Who Are We?

The Robotics Interest Group (RIG) was founded out of a passion for robotics, aiming to foster learning and research in areas like Mechatronics, Intelligent Systems, and Automation. We focus on gaining hands-on experience and knowledge to keep pace with evolving technologies, support industry innovation, and contribute to developing practical, commercially viable solutions.

Join us to explore the world of robotics, collaborate on exciting projects, and push the boundaries of technology.
